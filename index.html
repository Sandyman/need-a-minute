NEED A MOMENT?

Do you need a moment?

We all do, sometimes. A moment to think something over. To reflect. To be in an environment where we can truly think for ourselves. I can provide you that environment in a setting of your choice. You pick the spot. Your favourite coffee place, or a nice room in your office. The place where you can feel comfortable. And I give you my fullest attention, so you can think. Really think. Free of unwanted distractions and free of judgement.

Quite the proposition, don't you think?

How do we start?

Let me know that you want to have a moment by filling out the form at the bottom of this page, and we'll take it from there.

Read More

How does it work?

We start the session with a simple question that will spark your thinking. When your thinking subsides, a follow-up question will re-ignite it. Rinse and repeat until you're done.

Read More

What does it cost?

A regular session is $200 (plus 10% GST) for a maximum of one hour. This includes the time to get set up. Prepaid sessions are discounted by 15%. Conditions apply.
